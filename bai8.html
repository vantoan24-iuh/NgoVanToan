A smile is a facial expression formed primary by plexing the muscles at the sides of the mouth. It is
belived it takes about 80 muscles to smile. Some smiles include a contraction of the muscles at the corner of the eyes,
an action known as a "Duchenne smile". Smiles performed without the eye contraction may be perceived as insincere.
