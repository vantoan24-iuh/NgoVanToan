One particular book which is recommended
reading is The Street Lawyer by John Grisham.
This book is about a lawyer who begin re-
evaluating his priorities in life when a bad
incident occurs within his law firm. Consequently,
he becomes acquainted with the inner city streets,
and realizes the hash existence of the homeless,
and wows to give them a chance in the courts. The
Strees Lawyer is a great book. It is well Written
and interesting. Orther books by Jonh Grisham
include The Film, The Pelican Brief, and The
Cliet.
